Hi! I'm Lauren, a product designer and visual artist from SoCal

I'm currently studying Human-Computer Interaction and DP3: Drawing, Painting, Print Media, and Photography at Carnegie Mellon University. I love harmonizing human-centered design, evolving technology, and visual arts to tell meaningful stories. As we become more aware of how entangled technology is in our lives, I increasingly see human-centered design as a solution to creating digital products that are inclusive, useful, and memorable.

You can also find me singing to Joji, taking film photos, and watching anime.

I'm always looking for new projects. Feel free to check out my resume or reach me via ljpark@andrew.cmu.edu.
